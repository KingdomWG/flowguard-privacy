FlowGuard Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 16, 2026

Overview

FlowGuard is a capture-first task app. Users can type or speak tasks, and FlowGuard helps sort them into Today, Inbox, or a quick review flow.

Information We Collect

  • Sign in with Apple account information needed to create and maintain your account.
  • Account status information such as whether your account is on the free plan or Pro plan.
  • Daily AI usage statistics used for quota enforcement, abuse prevention, and operational support.

Information We Do Not Store in the AI Quota Backend

  • Your task or capture text.
  • Your prompt bodies.
  • Your AI response bodies.

The current production design stores usage statistics for quota enforcement and operations, but does not store task text, prompt bodies, or AI response bodies in the AI quota backend.

How FlowGuard Uses Third-Party Services

  • Supabase is used for authentication, account bootstrap, and quota-related account records.
  • Anthropic is used for server-side AI parsing. FlowGuard sends AI parsing requests through a server-side service. API keys are not stored in the client app.

Usage Limits

  • Free plan: 3 AI uses per day.
  • Pro plan: 30 AI uses per day.

How We Use Your Information

  • To let you sign in and access your account.
  • To determine whether you can use AI features.
  • To enforce daily usage limits.
  • To keep the service reliable and secure.

Data Sales and Model Training

FlowGuard does not sell your personal data. The AI quota and statistics backend is not designed to store your task content for model training.
